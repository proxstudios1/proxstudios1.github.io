About Pro X Studios

Pro X Studios is a Rajasthan-based brother duo creating original,
genre-blending music for a global audience.

We fuse Rajasthani traditional elements, Hindi and Punjabi lyricism,
with hip-hop, cinematic and world music textures.

Our focus is authentic sound, powerful lyrics,
and world-class music production.
